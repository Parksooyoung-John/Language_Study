# HSKK Intermediate Format

## Exam Structure

| Part | Task | Items | Time | Study focus |
| --- | --- | ---: | ---: | --- |
| 1 | Listen and repeat | 10 | 5 min | listening retention, tones, sentence rhythm |
| 2 | Describe pictures | 2 | 4 min | observation, structure, connected speech |
| 3 | Answer questions | 2 | 4 min | opinion, reason, example, conclusion |

Total speaking items: 14  
Speaking time: 13 minutes  
Full test duration: about 23 minutes including preparation  
Score: 100 full score, 60 passing

## Practice Rules

- Part 1 practice should prioritize exact repetition before speed.
- Part 2 answers should follow: scene -> people/actions -> details -> possible story/opinion.
- Part 3 answers should follow: direct answer -> reason -> example -> closing sentence.
- Every practice output should include timing guidance and a short self-score checklist.

## Feedback Categories

| Category | What to check |
| --- | --- |
| Pronunciation and tones | wrong tones, unclear initials/finals, unnatural stress |
| Repetition accuracy | missing words, changed word order, lost particles |
| Grammar | sentence completeness, aspect markers, measure words |
| Vocabulary | variety, appropriateness, HSK 3-4 range |
| Fluency | long pauses, restarts, fillers |
| Task completion | answer length, relevance, clear structure |
